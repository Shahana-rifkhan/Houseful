Our History

From a humble start in sales to a beloved Sri Lankan brand, HOUSEFUL has grown through dedication to craft, respect for materials, and a deep love for design.

The story began within one of Sri Lanka’s leading furniture suppliers, where skills were honed on the shop floor, understanding customers, materials, and the rhythm of a thriving showroom.

That foundation led to overseeing the journey from timber log to showroom and developing a complete view of the craft.

In 2018, the first HOUSEFUL showroom opened under a name gifted by a close friend. The concept “to make a house complete” resonated quickly: authentic in Sri Lankan identity, blended with modern and traditional designs.

Business grew steadily until the unforeseen challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic prompted a shift in operations. HOUSEFUL refocused its craft from a dedicated base in Moratuwa, Sri Lanka’s furniture capital, where work continues today with the same commitment to quality, detail, and service that shaped the brand from the start.

  • Early Years: Sales → Merchandising → Showroom leadership
  • 2018: HOUSEFUL showroom opens; brand gains rapid trust
  • 2020: COVID-19 disruption; operations restructured
  • Now: Crafting from Moratuwa with a focus on custom, Sri Lankan-identity design

Through every chapter, the purpose remains unchanged: We strive to engrave the name of HOUSEFUL in the hearts of every customer in happiness to pass on our creations to their generations, with furniture that feels personal, lasts for generations, and carries the unmistakable character of Sri Lanka.
